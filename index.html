Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

InstaHiiT allows you to measure and track your High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workouts metrics and data it is not transmitted outside of the app.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

No Personally-Identifying Information is transmitted to external locations when using InstaHiiT.

HealthKit Data

If you choose to have InstaHiiT integrate with HealthKit then the application will have access only to the health data types you opt-in to access.

The health data read from HealthKit is used for display within the app. It is not collected or disclosed outside of your local installation of the app. Only those data types relevant to the app are accessed.

InstaHiiT requests permission to write certain data types into your HealthKit data store. These are used when you perform a workout from InstaHiiT on an Apple Watch.

In no case is your health data ever transferred to a 3rd party. It is only ever kept within the application container of InstaHiiT or transfered to your personal HealthKit data storage as per your permission choices.
